## About BioFit

BioFit is a probiotic supplement featuring seven clinically-studied bacterial strains. The formula is designed to support gut health while promoting healthy weight management through improved digestion.

## How It Works

The probiotic strains work together to support gut health:

1. **Microbiome Balance** - Multiple strains support diverse gut bacteria
2. **Digestive Efficiency** - Helps break down food and absorb nutrients
3. **Metabolic Support** - Gut health influences metabolism and weight
4. **Bloating Reduction** - Balanced gut flora helps reduce gas and bloating

## Who Should Use BioFit

BioFit is perfect for:
- Those wanting to support gut health
- People experiencing bloating or digestive discomfort
- Anyone interested in the gut-weight connection
- Those who have taken antibiotics recently

## Recommended Usage

Take one capsule daily with water. Can be taken with or without food. For best results, take consistently for at least 60 days.
