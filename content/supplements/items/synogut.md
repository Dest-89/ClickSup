## About SynoGut

SynoGut is a comprehensive digestive support formula combining natural fibers, probiotics, and detoxifying agents. The formula is designed to support overall gut health and regularity.

## How It Works

The formula supports digestion through multiple approaches:

1. **Fiber Support** - Psyllium and oat bran provide soluble and insoluble fiber
2. **Probiotic Balance** - L. Acidophilus supports healthy gut bacteria
3. **Gentle Detox** - Bentonite clay helps bind and eliminate toxins
4. **Digestive Comfort** - Aloe vera soothes the digestive tract

## Who Should Use SynoGut

SynoGut is ideal for:
- Those experiencing occasional digestive discomfort
- Anyone wanting to support gut health naturally
- People needing more fiber in their diet
- Those seeking regular, comfortable digestion

## Recommended Usage

Take two capsules daily with a full glass of water. Best taken with meals. Increase water intake while using this product.
